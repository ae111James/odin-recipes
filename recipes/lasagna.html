Traditional Italian Lasagne

Lasagne, lasagne. How I love thee! It is possibly one of the most loved foods in the whole wide world and understandably so. There is just something so sentimental about lasagne, so comforting. It evokes images of gatherings with family and friends all around the world. It is the sort of food that is like a big warm hug and so more-ish you want to keep digging in until you burst.

Meat Sauce

  • Onion, Garlic, Carrot and Celery
  • Beef
  • Canned tomato and tomato paste
  • Red wine
  • Seasonings - beef bouillon cubes, bay leaves, thyme, oregano, worcestershire sauce

White Sauce

  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese

For assembling

  • Lasagne sheets
  • Cheese

Layering Up!

  1. Smear a bit of meat sauce on the base first - stops the lasagna sheets from sliding around.
  2. Layer 1 - top with meat sauce and a bit of white sauce
  3. Layer 2 - lay out more lasagne sheets, then top with more meat sauce and more white sauce
  4. Layer 3 - repeat again, lasagne sheets, meat sauce then white sauce
  5. Topping - cover with lasagne sheets, pour over remaining white sauce then sprinkle with cheese
